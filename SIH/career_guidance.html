Career Guidance — Class-wise & Stream-wise

From Class 8 → Class 12 (Science / Commerce / Arts / Technology) → After College. Click any exam to open its official website.

After Class 8 (early options)
  • General:
  • Navodaya Vidyalaya Samiti (JNVST)
  • Sainik School / AISSEE
  • School Olympiads (SOF) — early talent tests
After Class 9
  • Scholarship & Olympiads
  • NTSE — Stage I prep / NCERT info
  • SOF Olympiads (IMO, NSO)
  • National Science Olympiad (NSO - SOF)
After Class 10
  • General & Vocational
  • Polytechnic / technical approvals (AICTE) — state polytechnic portals vary
  • ITI / NCVT (vocational trades)
  • Navodaya / Sainik School (if still applicable)
  • NTSE (Stage I / scholarships)
Class 11 — Science (what to aim for)
Major entrance / prep sites
  • JEE Main (NTA)
  • JEE Advanced (IITs)
  • NEET (NTA - Medical)
  • CSIR NET (research exam — later)
  • Science Olympiads (SOF/HBCSE pathway)
Class 11 — Commerce
Routes & early prep
  • CA Foundation (ICAI) — start planning early
  • CS (ICSI) — Company Secretary pathway
  • CMA (ICMAI)
  • CUET — university admissions (commerce programs)
Class 11 — Arts / Humanities
  • CUET — entry to many BA / humanities programs (NTA)
  • Language & literature olympiads / college-specific entrance tests (look at target universities)
  • Law pathway — prepare for CLAT (from 12th, but early reading helps)
Class 11 — Technology / Coding
  • NIELIT (DOEACC) — O/A/B/C level & IT certifications
  • C-DAC professional courses & C-CAT (PG / Diploma)
  • National coding competitions / robotics olympiads — many organisers (state & private)
After Class 12 — Science
  • JEE Main (NTA)
  • JEE Advanced
  • NEET (UG) — NTA
  • BITSAT (BITS Pilani)
  • VITEEE (VIT)
  • AIIMS / AIIMS exams portal
After Class 12 — Commerce
  • CA Foundation (ICAI)
  • CS Foundation (ICSI)
  • CMA Foundation (ICMAI)
  • CUET — B.Com / BBA / university entries
  • Bank exams (IBPS) — later recruitment paths
After Class 12 — Arts
  • CUET — BA / B.Ed / Humanities
  • CLAT (Law) — Consortium of NLUs
  • Design / Fashion — NIFT
  • Design — NID
  • Hospitality (NCHM JEE) / Hotel Management
After Class 12 — Technology
  • Engineering routes (JEE Main / Advanced)
  • NIELIT / DOEACC levels
  • C-DAC PG diplomas (C-CAT info on CDAC)
  • State / Institute-specific tech diplomas & polytechnic entries (check AICTE / state DTE)
After College — Major national paths
General / Civil Services
  • UPSC — Civil Services & many national exams
  • SSC (CGL, other recruitment)
  • IBPS / Banking recruitment
Science / Engineering (PG)
  • GATE — M.Tech / PSUs (organiser page varies by year)
  • CSIR NET (JRF / Lectureship)
  • C-DAC courses / C-CAT
Commerce / Management / Law
  • ICAI / CA (post-grad career route)
  • CAT — MBA (IIMs & other B-schools)
  • CLAT (PG & UG law routes)
Tip: many exams have state or institute-specific portals (eg. polytechnic admissions). For any specific exam you want prioritized on the site, I can add direct deep links to application pages or live widgets.
